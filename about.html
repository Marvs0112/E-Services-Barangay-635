History of Barangay 635

Barangay 635 is a barangay in the city of Manila, under the administrative district of Sampaloc. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 660. This represented 0.04% of the total population of Manila.

Demographics
Households The household population of Barangay 635 in the 2015 Census was 716 broken down into 152 households or an average of 4.71 members per household.

Population
by age group According to the 2015 Census, the age group with the highest population in Barangay 635 is 20 to 24, with 87 individuals. Conversely, the age group with the lowest population is 75 to 79, with 3 individuals.

age groups together, those aged 14 and below, consisting of the young dependent population which include infants/babies,

The computed Age Dependency Ratios mean that among the population of Barangay 635, there are 35 youth dependents to every 100 of the working age population; there are 10 aged/senior citizens to every 100 of the working population; and overall, there are 45 dependents (young and old-age) to every 100 of the working population.The median age of 27 indicates that half of the entire population of Barangay 635 are aged less than 27 and the other half are over the age of 27.

Historical population
The population of Barangay 635 fell from 862 in 1990 to 660 in 2020, a decrease of 202 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a negative growth rate of 1.70%, or a decrease of 56 people, from the previous population of 716 in 2015.
