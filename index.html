what makes our website special?

The primary purpose of Barangay 635 E-Services is to streamline and modernize community interactions by providing a centralized online platform.

This website aims to simplify and expedite processes related to document processing, payment transactions, and information dissemination, fostering a more efficient and digitally connected Barangay experience.

VISION MISSION GOAL
A Barangay that is God-centered, competent, orderly, honest, peaceful, credible, gender responsive, and abides by the Code of Conduct.
We exercise transparency, integrity, professionalism, efficiency, and most of all, we conduct free services because as Public Servants. We are accountable to the residents of Barangay 635.
Barangay 635 aims to be efficient in serving the public because Public Office is a Public Trust and must at all times be accountable to the people, serve them with utmost responsibility, loyalty and efficiency, act with patriotism and justice and lead modest lives.

Meet Hon. Danilo San Bueno: A Dedicated Leader Serving Barangay 635


Hon. Danilo San Bueno, a two-term Barangay Kapitan of Barangay 635 and an alumnus of Eulogio Amang Rodriguez Institute (EARIST), prioritizes collaboration between universities and barangays, leveraging technology for community welfare.

Under his leadership, Development and Humanitarian Programs (DHP) continue to support Barangay 635, emphasizing sustainable development and empowerment.

Hon. San Bueno advocates for senior citizens and LGBTQ+ rights, fostering inclusivity and support for students. Known for his balanced approach, he ensures security and unity within the barangay, promoting harmony and prosperity.

Barangay 635 thrives under Hon. San Bueno's leadership, embodying progress, inclusivity, and community welfare.
